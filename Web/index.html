Analyse your performance

Turn Up gives an option to the users to view the lectures they have attended in the past and analyse their performances in each module.

Tap and Attend

It’s an extremely easy system for Universities to implement and for students to use, Just with a tap with their phone the user can register his/her attendance. No more wasting time by calling out names or passing a device to register your attendance.

About TurnUp


Turn Up is an android application for universities, which makes registering student attendance process easier and faster. Simply by placing an android phone on a NFC chip in a lecture hall or seminar room allows the students to register their attendance. It also lets the students view their upcoming lectures and the list of the lectures they have attended in the past.
